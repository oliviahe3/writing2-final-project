Introduction

Shanghai is one of the largest cities in China, and also a really popular tourist destination. This city is special to me because it is very significant to my culture and family background. Both my parents grew up in China and all of my extended family and relatives live there. So every summer my family and I will travel to China to visit our family.

Shanghai is such a lively, vibrant, and exciting city, and there are endless possibilities of things to do there. I have a lot of great memories from my time there and even though I feel a sense of comfort and familiarity, I always look forward to going back and exploring more.

My favorite aspect about Shanghai is definitely the food! During the rest of the year, you can always find my mouth watering as I dream about all the iconic dishes. Now that I’m back in Shanghai for the summer, take a look as I bring you guys through a day of must try foods in the city.
